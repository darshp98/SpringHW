Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

  1. One stick of unsalted butter
  2. 3/4 cup of packed dark brown sugar
  3. 3/4 cup of sugar
  4. Two large eggs
  5. One teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
  6. One 12 ounce bag of semisweet chocolate chips
  7. 2 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  8. 3/4 teaspoon of baking soda
  9. One teaspoon of fine salt
